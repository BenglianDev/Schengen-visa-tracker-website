How to Use Schengen Visa Tracker

1

Getting Started - The Home Screen

When you first open the app, you'll see the main Tracker screen. This is your dashboard showing your current Schengen visa status at a glance. The progress ring shows how many of your 90 days you've used, and the cards below show your remaining days and when you'll next have full availability.

2

Recording Your Trips

Tap the blue + button at the bottom of the screen to add a trip. You'll see two options: "Record a Trip" for trips that have happened or are currently happening, and "Plan a Trip" for quick future trip calculations. Select "Record a Trip" to log your past Schengen visits.

3

Quick Trip Planning

Use "Plan a Trip" from the home screen for a quick check on whether a future trip fits within your allowance. Enter your proposed dates and instantly see if you'll have enough days. This is perfect for checking before you book flights!

4

Calendar View - See Your Travel History

Tap the Calendar tab to see a visual overview of all your recorded trips. Days spent in the Schengen Area are highlighted in blue. Toggle between Month and Year views using the buttons at the top. Each day shows your remaining Schengen days for that date.

5

Planning Tab - Manage Future Trips

The Planning tab lets you add and manage multiple future trips. Unlike the quick "Plan a Trip" feature, trips added here are saved and shown on the calendar in purple. This helps you plan your entire travel year and see how each trip affects your remaining days.

6

Understanding the Calendar Display

Blue days = Past trips (recorded Schengen visits)
Purple days = Planned future trips
Numbers on each day = Your remaining Schengen days as of that date
Green numbers = Plenty of days remaining (60+)
Orange numbers = Getting low (30-59)
Red numbers = Critical or exceeded (below 30 or negative)

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 20, 2024

Data Collection

Schengen Visa Tracker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All trip data is stored locally on your device only. We believe your travel history is personal information that should remain private.

Data Storage

Your travel records are saved using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties. Even we cannot see your trips or travel patterns.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We don't use Firebase, Google Analytics, or any other tracking tools. The only external service used is Google Play for processing purchases, which is handled entirely by Google according to their privacy policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed through Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information. Google handles all billing securely according to their own privacy and security standards.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data in your device settings. Since no data is stored on our servers, uninstalling the app permanently removes all your information.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
